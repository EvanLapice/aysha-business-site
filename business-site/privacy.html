Privacy Policy

Effective Date

September 17, 2025

Aysha Lapice (“we,” “us,” or “our”) respects your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information when you use our appointment booking form or related services.

1. Information We Collect

When you book an appointment, we collect the personal information you voluntarily provide, including:

  • Name
  • Email address
  • Phone number
  • Appointment preferences or notes you submit

We do not collect sensitive personal information beyond what is necessary to schedule your appointment.

2. How We Use Your Information

  • Schedule and manage your appointment
  • Send confirmations, reminders, and follow-up communications
  • Respond to your inquiries

We do not use your information for unrelated marketing without your consent.

3. No Sale or Sharing of Your Data

We do not sell, rent, trade, or share your personal information with third parties for their marketing purposes. Your data remains within ayshaempowers.netlify.app and is only accessed by authorized personnel who need it to perform the Services.

4. Communications

By providing your phone number and/or email address, you consent to receive messages related to your appointment (confirmations, reminders, updates). Standard message and data rates may apply. You may opt out at any time by replying “STOP” to SMS messages or unsubscribing from emails.

5. Data Retention

We retain your information only as long as needed to provide the Services or as required by law. Afterward, we securely delete or anonymize your data.

6. Security

We take reasonable administrative, technical, and physical measures to protect your personal information from unauthorized access, use, or disclosure.

7. Your Rights

Depending on your jurisdiction, you may have the right to access, correct, or request deletion of your personal information. To exercise these rights, contact us at: AyshaEmpowers@proton.me.

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. When we do, we will post the revised policy on this page with a new effective date.

9. Contact Us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy or our data practices, please contact us at the email above.

By submitting your information through our appointment form, you acknowledge you have read and understood this Privacy Policy.
